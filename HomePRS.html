PRS - A Step Towards Better Government.

1. Bharatmala: The development of any nation depends on the transportation networks and the ways in which they are being maintained. The same holds true for the development of a huge and populous nation like India.

2. Sagarmala: Vision of the Sagarmala Programme is to reduce logistics cost for EXIM and domestic trade with minimal infrastructure investment. As part of Sagarmala Programme, more than 610 projects (Cost: Rs. 7.78 Lacs Cr.)

3. Inland Waterways: Inland Waterways Authority of India (IWAI), anticipating an increase in cargo traffic, plans to purchase 8-10 inland cargo vessels to operate on two key waterways – the Ganga and the Brahmaputra.

3. Mumbai Trans Harbour Link, Shivaji Memorial: The proposed Mumbai Trans Harbour Link (‘MTHL') has been planned to facilitate decongestion of the island city by improving connectivity between Island city and main land (Navi Mumbai) and development of Navi Mumbai Region.
